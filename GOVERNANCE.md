# Governance

The Steward Project belongs to everyone who implements it.

## Principles of governance

- **Open** — Specifications are public and free to implement.
- **Independent** — Not controlled by any single company, including the one that
  wrote the first draft.
- **Community-driven** — Changes to the Standard are proposed, debated, and
  adopted in the open.
- **Consent-based** — No requirement is added that strips human agency.

## How decisions are made (target state)

- Proposals are issued as pull requests against this repository.
- Significant changes to `STANDARD.md` require a public comment period.
- A future **Steward Foundation** (nonprofit) holds the trademark and coordinates
  voting, with no commercial veto.

## Today

This repository is seeded by its early stewards. Until the Foundation forms, the
community reviews and challenges changes openly. Compatibility badges are awarded
and revoked by community consensus, not by a vendor.

> Many implementations. One shared foundation.
