# AGENTS.md

You are operating inside **The Steward Project** repository — an open protocol
for collaborative intelligence, not a product and not a startup.

## Before any change

1. Read [`CONSTITUTION.md`](CONSTITUTION.md), [`LANGUAGE.md`](LANGUAGE.md), and
   [`STANDARD.md`](STANDARD.md).
2. Run the **Constitutional Review** in
   [`review/CONSTITUTIONAL_REVIEW.md`](review/CONSTITUTIONAL_REVIEW.md) against
   your proposed change. Do not merge or certify anything that fails it.
3. Ask the standing question on every edit: *"Does this still reflect the
   constitution?"*

## Non-negotiables

- **Standards effort, not a startup.** The protocol must be adoptable by anyone,
  anywhere.
- **AetherTwin is one implementation, not the project.** Never require it. Never
  imply adoption depends on it.
- **No lock-in.** Keep everything model-agnostic and OS-agnostic. No vendor
  account required to benefit.
- **Words before software.** When unsure whether to add spec or build tooling,
  add the words.
- **Local-first where possible; no hidden net; no extraction.** These are
  Principles 5, 6, and the Fairy OS laws.

If a proposed change cannot pass the Constitutional Review, revise it or stop —
and say why.
